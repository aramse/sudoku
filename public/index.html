      Sudoku

      Fill the 9x9 grid with the given digits
      or pictures so that each row, column
      and 3x3 subgrid contains all of
      the given options exactly once.


      Levels

      Choose on of the 5 available Levels.
      Easy, Advanced, Hard are standard Sudokus
      Kids uses pictures instead of digits
      and Extreme will make your life miserable.


      Modi & Scores

      Its a race against the clock,
      be fast to make it on the scoreboard.
      To help make your life easier you can:
      Switch options with your keyboard,
      Erase set digits/pictures,
      Activate the helper mode,
      and look at the final solution.
